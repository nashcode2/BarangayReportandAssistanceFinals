## üöÄ Getting Started

### First Time Setup

1. **Install the App**
   - Download from Google Play Store, or
   - Install APK file provided by your barangay

2. **Open the App**
   - Tap the app icon on your device
   - Wait for the app to load

3. **Grant Permissions**
   - The app will request permissions for:
     - **Camera**: To take photos for reports
     - **Location**: To add location to reports
     - **Storage**: To save images and files
     - **Notifications**: To receive updates
   - Tap "Allow" for all permissions

4. **Register or Login**
   - If you're new, tap "Register" to create an account
   - If you have an account, enter your email and password to login

## üë§ Account Management

### Registering a New Account

1. Tap "Register" on the login screen
2. Fill in the required information:
   - **Full Name**: Your complete name
   - **Email**: Your email address
   - **Phone Number**: Your contact number
   - **Password**: Create a strong password (at least 6 characters)
   - **Confirm Password**: Re-enter your password
   - **Address**: Your barangay address
3. Tap "Register"
4. Verify your email (if required)
5. You'll be automatically logged in

### Logging In

1. Open the app
2. Enter your **Email** and **Password**
3. Tap "Login"
4. You'll be taken to the home screen

### Forgot Password

1. On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password?"
2. Enter your email address
3. Tap "Send Reset Link"
4. Check your email for password reset instructions
5. Follow the link to reset your password

### Logging Out

1. Go to Profile/Settings (usually in menu or bottom navigation)
2. Tap "Logout"
3. Confirm logout
4. You'll be returned to the login screen

## üìù Reporting Issues

### How to Submit a Report

1. **Access Report Feature**
   - From home screen, tap "Report Issue" card, or
   - Navigate to "Report Issue" from menu

2. **Fill Out Report Form**
   - **Issue Type**: Select category (Infrastructure, Safety, Environmental, etc.)
   - **Description**: Describe the issue in detail
   - **Location**: 
     - Tap "Get Current Location" to use GPS, or
     - Manually enter address
   - **Photo**: 
     - Tap camera icon to take photo, or
     - Tap gallery icon to select existing photo
   - **Priority**: Select urgency level (Low, Medium, High)

3. **Submit Report**
   - Review all information
   - Tap "Submit Report" button
   - Wait for confirmation message

4. **Track Your Report**
   - Go to "My Reports" to see all your submissions
   - Each report shows:
     - Status (Pending, In Progress, Resolved)
     - Submission date
     - Issue type
     - Description preview

### Viewing Report Details

1. Go to "My Reports"
2. Tap on any report card
3. View full details:
   - Complete description
   - Photos
   - Location on map
   - Status history
   - Admin comments (if any)

### Report Statuses

- **Pending**: Report submitted, awaiting review
- **In Progress**: Barangay is working on the issue
- **Resolved**: Issue has been fixed
- **Rejected**: Report was not approved (with reason)

### Tips for Effective Reports

- ‚úÖ Take clear, well-lit photos
- ‚úÖ Provide detailed descriptions
- ‚úÖ Include accurate location
- ‚úÖ Select appropriate issue type
- ‚úÖ Set correct priority level

## üêõ Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### App Won't Open
- **Solution**: 
  - Restart your device
  - Uninstall and reinstall the app
  - Check device storage space

#### Can't Login
- **Solution**:
  - Check internet connection
  - Verify email and password
  - Use "Forgot Password" if needed
  - Contact barangay admin

#### Reports Not Submitting
- **Solution**:
  - Check internet connection
  - Verify all required fields are filled
  - Ensure photo is not too large
  - Try again after a few minutes

#### Notifications Not Working
- **Solution**:
  - Check app notification permissions in device settings
  - Enable notifications in app settings
  - Restart the app

#### Maps Not Loading
- **Solution**:
  - Check internet connection
  - Enable location services
  - Grant location permissions
  - Restart the app

#### Can't Download Certificate
- **Solution**:
  - Check internet connection
  - Ensure certificate is approved
  - Check device storage space
  - Try again later
